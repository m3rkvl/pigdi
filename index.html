Rules

  • Roll the dice and the number will be added to your current score.
  • If you roll a one, you lose your current score and the round switches to your opponent.
  • If you are happy with your current score, click on hold and save your current score. And the round will switch to your opponent.
  • Play until one of you have a score of at least 100.
  • When the game ends, if you want to play again, simply click on new round to keep playing.
  • If you want to start an entirely new game, click on new game.
